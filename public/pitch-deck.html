The Problem

Discovering great food nearby is broken.

📷

Stale Content

Restaurant listings show stock photos and menus from months ago. Consumers can't see what's actually being served today.

💰

Commission Trap

Platforms like Deliveroo and Just Eat take 20–30% commission on every order, destroying margins for small businesses.

📈

Algorithm Gatekeeping

Instagram buries local businesses under algorithmic noise. Organic reach is near zero unless you pay to play.

🔍

No Dish-Level Search

Want to find the best fish & chips nearby? Google shows restaurants, not dishes. You can't search for food itself.

02 / 14

The Solution

A live food feed where businesses post real photos of real dishes — and consumers discover what's being served right now.

  • Real-time content — fresh dish photos posted by businesses daily
  • Zero commission — businesses keep 100% of their customer relationships
  • Global food map — explore food culture anywhere in the world
  • Positive-only reactions — "I'd eat that" builds genuine social proof
  • Dish-level discovery — search by dish, cuisine, or business

Not another review site.

A dedicated platform purpose-built for food businesses to showcase their craft — and for consumers to find their next meal.

Comments Q&A

Growth Strategy

Warm, inbound acquisition powered by real social proof.

1. Consumers Tag Venues

Users upload meal photos and tag where they ate. The platform aggregates mentions and reactions by venue — building social proof organically.

2. Warm Outreach

When a venue hits 3+ consumer posts or 20+ reactions, we reach out with real data: "Your dishes got 47 reactions this week."

3. Easy Onboarding

Businesses claim their profile, see existing social proof, and start posting. Pre-built audience from day one.
